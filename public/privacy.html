Bubby Privacy Policy

Bubby stores your profile, pantry, messages, preferences, and pet state locally in your browser using localStorage. You can clear this local data from the in-app info panel.

AI Requests

When you chat with Bubby, your message and a small amount of local context are sent to the Bubby API so an AI model can generate a reply. If you attach food photos, those images are sent for that request only.

Reports

If you report an AI response, Bubby sends only your report reason, the latest visible Bubby message, a timestamp, and a lightweight route label. Reports do not include your full chat history, images, profile, weight, or macro history.

Nutrition And Safety

Bubby is AI-generated and can be wrong. Bubby is not medical advice and is not a doctor, dietitian, therapist, or emergency service. Users with medical conditions, eating disorders, pregnancy, diabetes, or clinical nutrition needs should talk to a qualified professional.
