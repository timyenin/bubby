What Bubby Is

Bubby is an AI nutrition companion. It helps users chat about meals, nutrition targets, food photos, pantry items, and a small virtual pet experience.

Information Bubby Stores Locally

Bubby primarily stores app data on your device or browser using localStorage keys that start with bubby:. This local data may include:

  • profile and onboarding data, such as name, preferred name, age, height, weight, goals, activity level, training schedule, and rules
  • daily logs, meals, macro totals, weigh-ins, workout-day status, and adherence flags
  • pantry items and optional saved macros for pantry foods
  • Bubby state, such as vitals, animation state, sick/sleepy state, and last updated time
  • conversation history, including small stored image thumbnails or compressed local image copies attached to chat messages
  • long-term memory entries, such as preferences, rules, goals, schedule details, and other facts the user asks or allows Bubby to remember
  • local app preferences, such as onboarding completion, selected theme, selected Bubby color, and selected music option

Local data remains on your device or browser unless you clear it, clear browser/app data, uninstall the app, or use Bubby's in-app clear local data control.

Information Sent For AI Processing

When you send a chat message, Bubby sends your message and relevant app context to /api/chat so an AI response can be generated. This context may include recent conversation history, profile details, macro targets, daily nutrition totals, pantry information, Bubby state, long-term memory entries, current local time context, and safety signals such as calorie-floor or weight-loss-rate context.

Food Photo And Image Uploads

If you attach a food photo or other image, Bubby compresses and resizes the image in your browser before sending it for AI processing. Full-size upload images are used for that AI request. Bubby may keep smaller local thumbnails or compressed local image copies in chat history so the conversation can display the images later.

AI Response Reports

If you report an AI response inside the app, Bubby sends a minimal report to /api/report. The report includes only:

  • the reason you typed
  • the latest visible Bubby message, with hidden action envelopes removed
  • a timestamp
  • a lightweight route label

Reports do not send your full chat history, images, profile, weight, macro history, pantry, or memory list.

How Information Is Used

Bubby uses information to:

  • generate AI chat responses
  • estimate and log meals, macros, weight updates, workouts, and related nutrition context
  • maintain local conversation history, Bubby state, preferences, and memory
  • process optional food images when you choose to attach them
  • receive and review user reports about AI-generated responses during testing
  • operate, debug, secure, and improve app functionality

Third-Party Processors

Bubby uses server-side API endpoints to process AI requests. AI chat content and optional uploaded images may be sent to Anthropic/Claude for AI processing. Bubby may also rely on hosting and infrastructure providers to operate the app and API endpoints.

Data handled by service providers may be processed according to their systems, policies, and retention practices. This policy describes what Bubby's app currently sends and stores; it does not make guarantees beyond what the app controls.

Data Sharing

Bubby does not sell user data. Bubby does not currently use ads, billing, account sync, or an analytics SDK. Data may be shared with service providers only as needed to operate app functionality, such as AI processing, report handling, hosting, and server logs.

Data Retention And Deletion

Local Bubby data stays on your device or browser until you remove it. You can clear local Bubby data from the in-app info panel by selecting clear local data and confirming the prompt. This removes local bubby: storage keys and returns the app to onboarding.

Data sent to AI and report endpoints may be processed by service providers and may appear in server-side systems or logs according to those systems and policies. Bubby does not claim permanent deletion from third-party systems unless that deletion is separately supported and confirmed.

Security

Bubby uses HTTPS when served from production. No app can guarantee perfect security. Bubby does not claim end-to-end encryption, HIPAA compliance, or medical/clinical system status.

Children's Privacy

Bubby is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided information through Bubby, contact us using the contact email above.

AI Disclaimer

Bubby's responses are AI-generated and may be wrong, incomplete, or inappropriate for your situation. Verify important information before relying on it.

Health And Nutrition Disclaimer

Bubby is not medical advice. Bubby is not a doctor, dietitian, therapist, or emergency service. Users with medical conditions, eating disorders, pregnancy, diabetes, or clinical nutrition needs should talk to a qualified professional.

Changes To This Policy

This policy may be updated as Bubby changes. When the policy changes, the effective date should be updated and the new version should be made available from the app.
